The Mustard Tree

The Mustard Tree Foundation is a family of projects seeking to be a voice for the unheard. We strive to identify, support and empower the marginalised, misunderstood and overlooked in our community.

Projects

  • Rahab

    ‘Restoring All Hope And Belief’: awareness, support & empowerment – tackling sexual exploitation.

  • Lifeline

    Counselling for post-abortion, post-miscarriage & post-baby loss; crisis pregnancy advice & support.

  • Starting Point

    1-2-1 mentoring for 16-25 yr olds who are NEET.

  • Engage Befriending

    Localised befriending for isolated, elderly people in the Reading area.

  • Let's Grow

    A horticultural project for 16-25 yr olds who are NEET, improving work readiness.
